What is a Red Flat?

The residential apartment or flat in a building from where COVID-19 positive cases are detected is classified as a Red Flats.

SEALING OF RED FLATS

Only Red Flats in the building will be sealed Based on the approval received from RBP, De-suups will do the sealing. De-suups will visit and monitor to ensure the seal is not broken Sealing will be for a minimum 10 days
